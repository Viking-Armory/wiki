GlockSmith Block - For use with all Glock Models and Generations!

Have you have ever looked for something to hold your Glock connector while you make adjustments only to mar it with the tool you used? How about holding the firing pin while you try to get those tiny spring cups off? Do you hate it when your trigger shoe and trigger bar slide all over your work surface when you try to remove or replace the trigger shoe? Want to check and verify your trigger assembly before you drop it back into the frame?

Then this GlockSmith Block is for you!

With this sweet tool you can:

  * Remove and install Glock trigger shoes
  * Make Connector Adjustments
  * Have a Glock Trigger Jig. (Will NOT work with Glock Performance Trigger)
  * Remove and install firing pin spring and cup(s)
  * There is a cup area to hold your Glock small parts
  * You can use your factory pins to keep everything in place on the block
  * Fully compatible with our Glocksmith pins for sale HERE
