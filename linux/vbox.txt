**## Virtual Box CLI Commands ##**\\

**All Commands run without sudo**\\

#Check VM Status\\
\\
VBoxManage list vms -l | grep -e ^Name: -e ^State | sed s/\ \ //g | cut -d: -f2-\\
\\ 
# Start Server\\
\\
VBoxManage startvm "WebU" --type headless\\
\\
# Stop Server\\
\\
VBoxManage controlvm "WebU" poweroff --type headless\\
