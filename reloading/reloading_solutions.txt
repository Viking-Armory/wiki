====== Reloading Cleaning Solutions ======

**Brass Tumbler: (HF) In each tumbler drum I put:**

  *  1 ½ lb of stainless steel media (at the most) 
  *  About 1 lb of brass 
  *  Warm Water up to the lip or just below
  *  Lemi-shine (1/4 teaspoon) 
  *  Squirt of Dawn (enough that when you open the drum you see soap suds) 
  *  1/4 Cap Armor All Wax
  *  Tumble for 2 hours.

**Brass Tumbler: (FART)**

  *  5 lb of stainless steel media
  *  About 1 lb of brass 
  *  Warm Water up to the lip or just below
  *  Lemi-shine (1/4 teaspoon) 
  *  Squirt of Dawn (enough that when you open the drum you see soap suds) 
  *  1 Cap Armor All Wax
  *  Tumble for 3 hours.

**Ultrasonic RCBS Gun Cleaning Solution:**

  *   40:1 Ratio
  *   60oz water
  *   2oz cleaner (rounding up)
  *   Degas (only first time solution is mixed)
  *   Set to ~120 degrees F
  *   5 min for tools
  *   15 min for gun parts

**Ultrasonic Brass Cleaning:**

  *  Lemi-shine (1/4 teaspoon) 
  *  Squirt of Dawn (1/4 teaspoon)
  *  15 Minutes

**Simple Green Weapon Cleaning Solution:**

Simple Green Purple 30:1 ~4oz to 1 gallon

**Ballistol Weapon Lube:**

Ballistol Ultrasonic Cleaning Solution: 20:1 one gallon = 6.1 Ounces (oz)  I will round to 6 Ounces and top off to a gallon. 3 Ounces for a half gallon   90% Water 10% Balistol = ~13 oz per gallon\\
